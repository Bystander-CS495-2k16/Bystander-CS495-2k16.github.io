Bystander

A Police Recording Application for Android
This Police Recording application will allow users to create video recordings and upload them to their YouTube accounts, for safekeeping in case access to your device is lost. Bystander will also feature a section outlining the legal rights a person possesses when making video recordings of 3rd parties in public and private locations. Bystander will provide an easy to use interface and will offer users a robust and simple way to create and ensure the preservation of their recordings, while also supplying them with a clear overview of their legal rights.
